About us

In the summer of 2004, founders Juan Durin and Cam Seits set out to fufill their life-long dream of hiking the Appalachian trail together. Childhood friends from Charlotte, NC, Juan and Cam had since relocated across the country to Bend, OR and Burlington, VT. Hiking the 2,200 mile-trail from Springer Mountain in Georgia to Mount Katahdin in Maine — a journey that often takes more than six months — is no small feat, requiring meticulous planning to complete. Living on separate coasts across the continent made shopping for essential equipment like a tent nearly impossible. It was out of this need — to compare and shop high-quality tents from anywhere — that Abode was born, the first online tent marketplace.
